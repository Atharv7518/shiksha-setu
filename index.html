Career Guidance for Class 9 Students

Your journey to a successful career starts here. Explore your options and make informed decisions about your future.

Subjects in Class 9

In 9th grade, you will study a set of common subjects that build a foundation for your future studies. These typically include:

Mathematics
Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
Social Science (History, Geography, Civics, Economics)
English
Hindi/Regional Language

Choose a Stream for Higher Secondary (Class 11 & 12)

Science

The Science stream is ideal for students who have a strong interest in scientific theories, experiments, and a logical approach to problem-solving.

Commerce

The Commerce stream is suited for students who are interested in business, finance, economics, and mathematics.

Arts/Humanities

The Arts stream is for students who are creative, curious about human nature, and interested in social sciences, languages, and fine arts.

Career Options in Science

Engineering

Focuses on designing, building, and maintaining engines, machines, structures, and systems.

Top Colleges:

  • Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs)
  • National Institutes of Technology (NITs)
  • Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT)

Average Salary:

₹6 - ₹12 LPA (can be much higher for top graduates)

Medicine

Involves diagnosing, treating, and preventing diseases to improve human health.

Top Colleges:

  • All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS)
  • Christian Medical College (CMC), Vellore
  • Armed Forces Medical College (AFMC), Pune

Average Salary:

₹8 - ₹20 LPA (increases significantly with specialization and experience)

Career Options in Commerce

Chartered Accountant (CA)

Experts in accounting, auditing, taxation, and financial management.

Top Institutes:

  • The Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI)

Average Salary:

₹7 - ₹15 LPA (can go much higher with experience)

Business Administration (BBA/MBA)

Involves managing business operations, marketing, finance, and human resources.

Top Colleges:

  • Indian Institutes of Management (IIMs)
  • NMIMS, Mumbai
  • Christ University, Bangalore

Average Salary:

₹5 - ₹25 LPA (highly dependent on the college)

Career Options in Arts/Humanities

Civil Services (IAS, IPS)

Prestigious government jobs that involve public administration and policy implementation.

Preparation for:

  • Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) exams

Salary:

Starts around ₹56,100 per month + benefits

Journalism and Mass Communication

Involves reporting, writing, editing, and presenting news and information.

Top Colleges:

  • Indian Institute of Mass Communication (IIMC), Delhi
  • Symbiosis Institute of Media & Communication, Pune
  • Xavier Institute of Communications, Mumbai

Average Salary:

₹4 - ₹8 LPA
